Talks

Software Sustainability & the Carbon Aware SDK

August 2023

Women Impact Tech, Boston, MA

Many businesses today are looking to improve the sustainability of the software they build and run. This talk focuses on the different components of sustainability for software and how an open source project developed by the Green Software Foundation - the Carbon Aware SDK - is rising as a tool for one of those components.

Family Expectations & How they Impact Our Lives

October 2018

Multicultural Conference, MIT, Cambridge, MA
