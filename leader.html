HOLA New England Chapter Lead

Aug 2022 - present

Microsoft NERD, Cambridge, MA

HOLA (Hispanic and Latinx Organization of Leaders in Action) is a Microsoft employee resource group that provides professional and social networking for Microsoft full-time employees of, or interested in, the Hispanic & Latinx community. The New England Chapter encompasses all employees in the metro-Boston offices as well as remote employees across the New England region.

  • Grew employee participation by 75%, investing heavily in remote and secondary office connections.
  • Coordinated external speakers from local academia and coaching industry for employee development.
  • Expanded employee learning through curated heritage month celebrations.
  • Oversaw the efficient use of a quarterly budget ranging from $4,000-$5,000.

Lead Physics Instructor, Interphase EDGE

June-August, 2019-2022

Office of Minority Education, MIT, Cambridge, MA

The MIT Office of Minority Education's (OME) mission is to promote academic excellence, build strong communities, and develop professional mindsets among students of underrepresented minority groups, with the ultimate goal of developing leaders in the academy, industry, and society.

  • Led the physics team, including 2 instructors and 8 teaching assistants.
  • Coordinated all communication to students, from initial placement exams to standing exams for fall term.
  • Gathered data on student progress across all instructors and ran weekly meetings to discuss growth and potential issues.
  • Developed and taught introductory summer classroom and online physics curriculum, including lectures, problem sets, and exams.
